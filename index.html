vsHacks 2022

For Students, By Students

What is a Hackathon?

Our Hackathon is a week long competition, where teams of programmers and graphic designers come to create something cool! All abilities of programmers are welcome here, and there will be prizes for everyone (IF WE CAN GET STICKERS). In the Hackathon, there will be workshops and fun activities that you can participate in.

In short, this hackathon is both a competition to create something cool, and an event to have fun and meet new people!

The Details

Teams

What are the teams?
YOU, are the one in charge of teams! Grab some friends and form a team of up to four people! You can invite anyone you want, as long as they are a student living in Greater Vancouver

Don't have any friends who code?
You can also invite friends who are passionate about design, marketing, presenting, etc. as those are valuable skills to the hackathon as well! We can also help you find a team, where you can meet new people and make new friends!

  • How to build a Discord Bot

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Wait? A HACKathon? Isn't hacking illegal?

Yes, hacking in the sense of breaking into other people's computers is illegal. But being a hacker also means being an innovator and tinkerer, and that's what we hope this hackathon will encourage people to do.

Q. Is there an entry fee?

Nope! vsHacks is and will be completely free to participate in, although we might take donations in the future.

Q. Can i team up with a friend?

Of course you can! One of the reasons we started this hackathon is so we can create a platform where people with similar interests can take part in and create connections. We're allowing for teams of up to four people this year.

Q. Can I participate even if I am not a high school student in the vancouver area??

This hackathon is only for students in the greater vancouver area. Sorry!
