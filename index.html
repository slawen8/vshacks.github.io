About vsHacks

Meet New People

At vsHacks, you will have the unique opportunity to work with like-minded students from all across the Greater Vancouver area. Build a team of up to four people and start hacking!

Learn New Things

Regardless of your skill level, we're certain that you'll learn something new at vsHacks from our experienced mentors. From web development to Discord bots, our workshops have something to everyone. Check out our schedule for further details.

Win Awesome Prizes

Thanks to our sponsors, we're able to offer approximately $2000 dollars worth of software for all participants. A specific list of prizes is forthcoming.

Have Fun

Though our main focus is programming, we hope that you'll also have fun while doing so. To this end, we will be hosting several events and games outside of programming. Activities will range from games of Jackbox, to a Minecraft!
