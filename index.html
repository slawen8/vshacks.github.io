vsHacks 2022

For Students, By Students

What is a hackathon?

Our hackathon is a week long competition, where teams of programmers and graphic designers come together to create something cool! All abilities of programmers are welcome here, and there will be prizes for everyone. In the hackathon, there will be workshops and fun activities that you can participate in. At the end, there will be prizes for several winners, and prizes for all participants as well!

In short, this hackathon is both a competition to create something cool, a place to meet new people, and a place to have fun right before summer break!

The Details

Teams

What are the teams?
YOU, are the one in charge of teams! Grab some friends and form a team of up to four people! You can invite anyone you want, as long as they are a student living in Greater Vancouver

Don't have any friends who code?
You can also invite friends who are passionate about design, marketing, presenting, etc. as those are valuable skills to the hackathon as well! We can also help you find a team, where you can meet new people and make new friends!

Activities

What workshops will there be?
There will be all sorts of workshops for you. If you are new to coding, or unfamiliar with hackathons, these are for you!

  • Hackathon Project Ideas
  • How to build a Discord Bot
  • How to present your final product, and how to win!


What other activities will there be?
